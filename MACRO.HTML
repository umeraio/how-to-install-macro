Deployment Instructions

1. Open Word: Press Alt + F11 to open the VBA Editor.

2. Create Module: Right-click on 'Normal' > Insert > Module.

3. Paste Code: Copy your personalized macro below and paste it into the window.

4. Save Project: Press Ctrl + S inside the VBA window to save.

5. Save Document: Save your Word file as a Word Macro-Enabled Document (*.docm).
